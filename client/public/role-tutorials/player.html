Player

Members with the PLAYER role (and COACH at the same permission tier) browse the club roster, follow tournaments, and manage their own profile. Use + Match to log a casual match: you stay on one side of the pairing and confirm with the opponent’s password before entering scores.

Organizer tutorial Administrator tutorial

1. Sign in

Use the same login screen as everyone else. After sign-in you see the main tabs (Players, Tournaments, Statistics, History) without the admin-only system gear.

2. Players list and toolbar

The Players page loads the standard member list. Player accounts do not see + Player or + Tournament; + Match and Stats are available.

3. Record a match

Choose + Match, pick an active opponent (you cannot remove yourself from the pairing), then complete the opponent-password step before scores when the flow requires it.

Permissions at a glance

View tournaments, statistics, and history
Edit tournament results for matches you are in (password may be required)
Edit your own profile and password
Create tournaments or add members
Edit anyone else’s profile, roles, rating, or active status
Open System settings
